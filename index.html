My Projects

Technica Research

A group collaboration done at Technica Hackathon 2024 at the University of Maryland, we used data anonymization to mask as much participant information as possible from a behavioral study in Liberia. We compared several de-identification workflows and used data visualization to track risk–utility and confirm consistency across tools.


Tech: ARX, R, R Studio, sdcMicro

Beauty Shop Model

Developed a data-driven plan for a prospective beauty supply store near the University of Maryland by analyzing foot-traffic and revenue patterns to assess viability. It identified peak shopping times and clear interest in specialized hair care, which guided plans for location, inventory, merchandising, promotions, and an in-store room-rental model for appointments.

Tech: Python, PowerBI, Tableau
